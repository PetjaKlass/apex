## Boilerplate (≈ 60 words)

Apex is a Life Operating System for solo founders and duos. It connects daily
action to a long-term vision: One Big Thing, identity-based habits, cinema-mode
deep focus, vision & goals, and reflection rituals — with momentum, levels and an
optional AI coach. Offline-first and private by design, EU-hosted, available on
web, mobile and desktop.

## Key messaging

- **Identity over instruction** — speaks to who you're becoming, not chores.
- **A system, not a toolbox** — opinionated, not a blank canvas.
- **Calm but ambitious** — premium, restrained, focused.
- **Private by design** — offline-first, EU-hosted, no ads; your data is yours.
- **Solo-built, ships everywhere** — one codebase: web, PWA, iOS, Android, desktop.
