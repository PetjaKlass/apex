## Pitfalls

- Don't ask people to upvote across platforms simultaneously.
- HN posts can be killed by mods — have a backup morning, don't repost same day.
- Day-2 traffic always dips. Normal. Don't panic.
- Acknowledge negative comments, take notes, move on.
