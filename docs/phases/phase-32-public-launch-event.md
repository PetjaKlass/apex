## Common Pitfalls

**1. ProductHunt timing** — Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PST. Your team must be aware (no surprises).

**2. HackerNews moderation** — sometimes posts get killed by moderators. Have a backup launch plan ready.

**3. X / HN / PH conflict** — don't ask people to vote/upvote on multiple platforms simultaneously. They'll feel pressured.

**4. Server load** — sudden traffic spike can break things. Pre-warm Vercel, scale Supabase if needed.

**5. Customer support overload** — 100+ new signups means 50+ support questions in 24h. Plan response.

**6. Negative comments** — don't argue. Acknowledge, take notes, move on.

**7. Imposter syndrome on launch day** — every founder feels this. Push through.

**8. Post-launch crash** — typical to have low Day 2 traffic. Don't panic, this is normal.

**9. Don't launch on a Friday** — weekend = traffic dropoff.

**10. Time zones matter** — most ProductHunt voters are US-based. Optimize for EST mornings.
